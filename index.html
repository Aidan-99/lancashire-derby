Instructions how to Play

Welcome to the Lancashire Derby!
Place your bets on your favorite horse and watch them

  • Explore the Races

    Browse upcoming races, view racecards, and check details like horse stats, jockeys, and odds.

  • Place Your Bet

    Select your horse, enter the amount you want to bet, and confirm your bet.

  • Watch the race and see if your horse wins!
  • Track Results & Winnings

    After the race, check your results and see if your horse placed. Winnings are usually added to your in-app wallet
